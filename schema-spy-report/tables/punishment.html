punishment

-1 rows


Description

A punishment (NOMIS calls these awards or sanctions) given on a charge where the outcome was CHARGE_PROVED. The duration or amount is not held here but on the linked punishment_schedule rows. ADDITIONAL_DAYS and PROSPECTIVE_DAYS punishments are the added days that feed sentence calculation via the adjustments service.
