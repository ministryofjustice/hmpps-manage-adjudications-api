reported_adjudications

-1 rows


Description

A submitted adjudication charge - the root record for everything that follows (hearings, outcomes and punishments). One row per charge, not per incident: an incident involving several prisoners, or several offences, produces several rows. Includes records migrated from NOMIS as well as those raised in DPS.
